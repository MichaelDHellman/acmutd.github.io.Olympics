  • ACM Education

    Preparing students for the workforce by teaching useful skills

ACM Education helps give students valuable skills needed for the workforce. While college degrees may be more theory based, we aim to give students knowledge not typically attained in the classroom. We run the Interview Prep Series, Mentor Program, and various technical workshops during the school year. Feel free to reach us at education@acmutd.co.

Interview Prep Series

Trying to land an internship? Most tech companies have both behavioral and technical interviews. Along with a cohort, our series will prepare you for both and help nail your next interview!

Applications open soon. This series runs every semester.

Mentor Program

Our program aims to match undergrad ECS students with upperlevel ECS students to pass on valuable advice and start a connection.

Applications open in the fall semester.

Technical Workshops

There is no application! Just be on the look out for our events.

We're The Association for Computing Machinery at UT Dallas

We're the official UT Dallas chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery, the largest computing society on the planet. We're focused on giving back to the engineering community at UTD through industry events, collaborations, social events, Hack_UTD, and more.

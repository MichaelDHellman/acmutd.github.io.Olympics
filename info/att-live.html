AT&T Technology Development Program

AT&T Live!

October 2018
AT&T Corporate Headquarters
Dallas, TX

Graduating in May 2019 and looking for full-time positions?

This year, AT&T is hosting an event for their Technology Development Program called AT&T Live! It will take place in the second week of October 2018 at AT&T's corporate headquarters in Dallas, TX. It’s an all-expenses paid trip where candidates will have a chance to connect with our top innovators and test-drive our latest technology. Also, you will have the opportunity to learn about our Development Programs straight from the experts. Not only will you network with some of AT&T’s senior leaders and technology gurus, but you might even leave with a job offer!

Please send your resume to comet.acm@gmail.com to apply, the deadline is August 1, 2018.

Qualifications
Technology Development Program — Software Development

  • Computer Science Undergraduate
  • Graduating May 2019
  • Minimum of 2 internships within field of study or minimum of 1 internship and Senior Capstone
  • Preferred GPA of 3.3 or higher
  • Leadership experience: Campus club, Eagle Scout, etc.
  • Technical aptitude, strong analytical skills, problem solver, team oriented
  • Successful completion of technical assessment with qualifying score.
  • Locations: Dallas, Atlanta, El Segundo, & Middletown

Technology Development Program — Engineering & Operations

  • STEM Undergraduate or Masters (CS, CIS, MIS, CE, IE, EE, & Math), ALL need to have some programming skills
  • Graduating May 2019
  • Minimum of 2 internships within field of study or minimum of 1 internship and Senior Capstone
  • Preferred GPA of 3.3 or higher
  • Leadership experience: Campus club, Eagle Scout, etc.
  • Technical aptitude, strong analytical skills, problem solver, team oriented
  • Dallas & Atlanta are primary focus
